# Customer Journey Map

## User Persona

**Name:** Rahul Sharma

**Role:** Business Analyst

**Goal:** Analyze customer ordering behavior and identify business trends to improve revenue and operational efficiency.

---

## Journey Stages

| Stage | User Actions | Pain Points | Dashboard Solution |
|--------|--------------|-------------|--------------------|
| Data Collection | Receives raw food ordering data | Large datasets are difficult to interpret | Centralized Tableau dashboard |
| Dashboard Access | Opens the dashboard | Finding KPIs manually is time-consuming | KPI cards provide instant metrics |
| Data Exploration | Filters by City, Cuisine, Meal Type, Age Group | Comparing multiple dimensions is difficult | Interactive filters and visualizations |
| Insight Generation | Identifies trends and business opportunities | Raw numbers lack business context | Charts and heatmaps reveal patterns |
| Decision Making | Shares insights with management | Manual report preparation | Dashboard simplifies reporting |

---

## Expected Outcome

- Faster business analysis
- Improved operational decisions
- Better customer understanding
- Increased revenue opportunities
