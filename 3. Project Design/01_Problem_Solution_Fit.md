# Problem-Solution Fit

## Project Title

Food Ordering Behaviour and Consumer Trends: A Structured Analysis of Choices and Habits

---

## Problem Overview

Online food delivery platforms generate a large amount of transactional data every day. Although businesses collect valuable information such as customer demographics, cuisine preferences, order values, delivery times, and ratings, extracting meaningful insights from this raw data is challenging.

Without proper analysis, businesses face difficulties in:

- Understanding customer ordering behaviour.
- Identifying high-demand cuisines.
- Tracking city-wise performance.
- Monitoring delivery efficiency.
- Recognizing peak ordering periods.
- Making informed business decisions.

---

## Proposed Solution

This project develops an interactive Tableau dashboard that transforms raw food ordering data into meaningful visual insights.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Analyze customer ordering trends
- Compare city-wise performance
- Identify popular cuisines
- Evaluate delivery performance
- Understand customer demographics
- Support strategic decision-making

---

## Stakeholders

### Business Analyst
Uses the dashboard to identify trends, customer behaviour, and revenue opportunities.

### Operations Manager
Monitors delivery performance and workload distribution across cities.

### Marketing Team
Analyzes customer preferences to design targeted promotional campaigns.

### Customers
Benefit indirectly through improved service quality, faster delivery, and better food recommendations.

---

## Business Value

The dashboard helps organizations:

- Improve operational efficiency
- Increase customer satisfaction
- Optimize marketing strategies
- Enhance revenue generation
- Enable data-driven decision-making

---

## Expected Outcome

An interactive analytics solution that converts food ordering data into actionable business insights for multiple stakeholders.
