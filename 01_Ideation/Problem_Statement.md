# Problem Statement

## Project Title
Food Ordering Behaviour and Consumer Trends: A Structured Analysis of Choices and Habits

---

## Background

The rapid growth of online food delivery platforms has resulted in massive volumes of customer transaction data. Although this data contains valuable business information, organizations often struggle to extract actionable insights that support strategic decision-making.

Factors such as cuisine preferences, customer demographics, meal timings, delivery efficiency, city-wise demand, and revenue patterns significantly influence business performance. Without proper analysis, businesses cannot accurately identify customer behaviour, optimize operational efficiency, or improve customer satisfaction.

---

## Problem

Businesses need an interactive analytics solution capable of converting raw food ordering data into meaningful visual insights that help stakeholders make informed decisions.

Currently, it is difficult to answer important business questions such as:

- Which cities generate the highest number of orders?
- Which cuisines are most preferred?
- Which meal type contributes the highest revenue?
- Which age groups place the most orders?
- How does delivery time affect customer experience?
- What are the ordering trends across different customer segments?

---

## Proposed Solution

Develop an interactive Tableau dashboard that performs comprehensive analysis of food ordering behaviour by visualizing:

- Customer ordering trends
- Cuisine demand
- Revenue contribution
- Delivery performance
- Customer demographics
- City-wise analysis
- Meal preferences

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify business opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze food ordering behaviour.
- Identify popular cuisines.
- Understand customer demographics.
- Evaluate city-wise demand.
- Measure revenue contribution.
- Analyze delivery performance.
- Improve operational efficiency using business intelligence.

---

## Expected Outcome

The project provides an interactive analytical dashboard that helps businesses improve decision-making, optimize delivery operations, enhance customer satisfaction, and maximize revenue.
