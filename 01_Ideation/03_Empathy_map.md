# Empathy Map

## User Persona

Business Analyst working in an Online Food Delivery Company

---

## Thinks

- Which cities perform the best?
- Which cuisine should be promoted?
- How can revenue be increased?
- Why are some cities underperforming?

---

## Feels

- Needs accurate insights
- Wants fast reporting
- Requires interactive dashboards
- Wants reliable KPIs

---

## Says

- "Show me the highest revenue city."
- "Which cuisine should we promote?"
- "How many orders came from Gen Z?"
- "How is delivery performance?"

---

## Does

- Monitors daily KPIs
- Reviews Tableau dashboards
- Shares reports with management
- Tracks customer behaviour
- Identifies business opportunities

---

## Pain Points

- Large datasets
- Difficult manual reporting
- Hidden customer patterns
- Slow business decisions

---

## Gains

- Faster decision making
- Better customer understanding
- Increased operational efficiency
- Revenue optimization
- Improved delivery performance
